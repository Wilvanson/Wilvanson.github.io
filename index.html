About Me

Ever since I was a child, I have always been interested in computers. Whether it's playing video games all day, hanging out on social media as a teenager, and discovering a passion for software development, I enjoy coding. Whether it's solving complex problems or finding an innate ability to design, so many people from different walks of life can come together through a common interest. This made the decision to study computer science easier. After four years of earning my BS in Computer Science, I also attended a coding boot camp called App Academy. Combining all teachings, I bring a deep knowledge of software development that I would love to bring to your organization. The 6-month App Academy Intensive Bootcamp helped me develop my communication skills and flexibility across platforms and build my confidence in my software development skills. I learned Java, JavaScript and Python. I have created several projects as well as a Spotify clone using a Python backend as well as a React/Redux frontend and a PostgreSQL database. Most recently I worked at a company called FDM Group and attended their training focused on Java, Angular and frameworks like Spring and Spring Boot. During that time, I've been working with other developers on a ticking system that allows employees to report issues they might be having with various issues like payroll and other employee issues.
